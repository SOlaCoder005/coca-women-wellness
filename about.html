About

Between 2015 and 2019 Sam attend the University of Greenwich, graduating with a 1st Class degree in BA Hons Public Relations & Communications. Once moving out of to another city in 2019, the pandemic hit, the world was in lockdown and around the world, social interactions were almost completely to the screen.

Such developments encouraged Sam to create Coca Women Wellness, an online platform designed to provide online communities with resources focused on promoting mindfulness practices and wellbeing

In line with her passion for African & Caribbean culture(s), health, and wellbeing, she decided to create the online platform - Coca Women Wellness. Her key aspiration and hopes for this site is to increase awareness of a variety of content that positively influences African & Caribbean culture, and the wellbeing of those within it.

Sam is currently working on several projects focusing on shedding more light developing the presence of African Caribbean culture in media, health and other social spheres
