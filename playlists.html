Our Playlists

Want to cook something healthy and delicious, learn some new ‘yogi’ techniques or even want to chill to some music while you ‘centre’? Below are some online videos that can help you on your mindfulness and wellness journey. We have also compiled tailored playlists with digital resources by wellness influencers to assist you in reaching your mindfulness goals!

Food

Not sure about you but we love food! Feeding our bodies is just as important as feeding our minds. This playlist provides healthy recipes that not only looks good but are nourishing too!

Body

We love a good stretch, do you? This playlist consists of videos that provides guidance on basic exercises (e.g. yoga) that you can use to develop your overall physical balance and wellbeing. Feel free to check them out, always start slowly and gradually elevate your skills!

Social

No one is a lone island. Positive social interactions with friends, family and peers are often great for our wellbeing. This playlist consists of content around both platonic, familial and romantic relationships.

Music

Who doesn’t love music? Whether we are having ‘me’ time, trying to create calm and tranquillity in your environment, or completing day-to-day tasks; this playlist provides a plethora of music that many have found these stress relieving.

Mind

Looking for new techniques to help you ‘centre’ and increase your overall equilibrium? Why not check out this playlist? We have collated several videos consisting of, but not limited to, guided meditation and relaxation.
