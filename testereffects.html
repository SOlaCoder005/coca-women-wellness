Gluten-free Pancakes

For some, gluten free food can help aid digestion and inflammation! With that in mind, these gluten free pancakes are a beautiful treat for anyone with a sweet tooth. At least we can feel less guilty about eating it as it is gluten free!

Ingredients

  • 250g gluten-free plain flour

    • ¼ cup of coconut sugar (use any sugar/sweetener of your choice)

    ¼ tsp Xanthan gum (optional, depending on whether the flour has a raising agent in it already)

    1 egg

    250ml Almond milk (use any milk/sweetener of your choice)

    Dairy free / gluten-free butter of your choice

    Honey

    Sliced strawberries (as much as you want as this is for the topping)

Instructions

  1. Turn your oven hob on and let the frying pan heat up to a medium heat.

    2. 2. Mix the milk, sugar, and egg together until fully combined

    3. Add the flour to the milk, egg, and sugar mixture. Mix well until fully combined.

    4. Once the frying pan is hot, add some butter to the pan and let it spread around the pan base.

    5. Add the pancake batter to the pan. Wait for the bottom of the pancake to brown and then flip.

    6. Once both sides are brown, remove the pancake from the pan and place on the plate.

    7. Repeat the process until the pancake batter has been used. Stack each pancake on top of each other.

    8. Drizzle honey over the pancakes and add strawberries on top for extra flavour!

    Eat and enjoy! Feel free to share your outcomes with us by taggin us on our social media!
